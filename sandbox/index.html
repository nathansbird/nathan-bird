12-18-2019

Chiang Mai, Thailand

Beauty in the Unexplained

On our third rest day after our transition to Thailand, several of us decided to make a trip out to the Sticky Waterfalls (or Bua Tong Waterfalls as it's actually called). After we climbed around the waterfall for a bit, we decided to go exploring. We found what we thought was a path leading to another beautiful waterfall and headed out.
